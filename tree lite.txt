### Why this layout (fast upgrade path, clean separation)

* **Admin/public/includes tri-layer** for clarity and performance; viewer lib lives under `public/lib/pannellum/`, mirroring WPVR’s proven bundling of Pannellum.&#x20;
* **Gutenberg block + Elementor widget** ship in Lite; additional builders (Divi, Oxygen, Bricks, VC) remain Pro-only, just like competitors split editor integrations. &#x20;
* **Limits enforcement** isolated in `class-vx-limits-lite.php` so Pro can disable it cleanly when active.
* **Import/Export, Duplicate, Lazy-load, Preview** included in Lite to maximize adoption; advanced analytics/floorplan/video reserved for Pro via upgrade notices.
* **Upgrade bridge** detects Pro plugin/constants and hands over feature flags without code churn.
